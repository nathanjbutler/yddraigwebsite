Y Ddraig Stoke-on-Trent and Staffordshire Viking re-enactment group

Y Ddraig are a Stoke-on-Trent re-enactment group, recreating a Viking longship’s crew of the C10-11th. We put on events for the public, including a living history display, recreating a traditional Viking camp on campaign, weapons displays, and of course the public favourite, large scale Viking re-enactment battles.

Y Ddraig are members of the larger national re-enactment society ‘The Vikings’ and take part in re-enactment events throughout the United Kingdom and abroad.

If you are interested in joining Y Ddraig or would like to book us for an re-enactment event please contact us here.
