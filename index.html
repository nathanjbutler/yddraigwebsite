Y Ddraig Stoke-on-Trent and Staffordshire Viking re-enactment group

Y Ddraig is a Stoke-on-Trent and Staffordshire based Viking re-enactment group focused on recreating a longship's crew of the 10-11th Century. We put on re-enactment events for the public, often including a living history setup that recreates a traditional Viking camp during a war campaign. Events also include weapons displays and the public favourite, large scale Viking re-enactment battles.

Y Ddraig are members of a larger national Viking re-enactment society 'The Vikings' and take part in re-enactment events throughout the United Kingdom and abroad.

If you are interested in joining Y Ddraig or would like to book us for a re-enactment event please contact us.
